What did you miss?

  • Urgency: The email claimed your password would expire in 24 hours to make you panic.
  • Generic Greeting: It said "Dear User" instead of your name.
  • Suspicious Link: The login page URL was not the official company domain.

How to stay safe

  • Verify Sender: Always check the actual email address, not just the display name.
  • Hover Links: Hover over buttons to see the real URL before clicking.
  • Don't Rush: Scammers use urgency to trick you. Take a moment to think.
